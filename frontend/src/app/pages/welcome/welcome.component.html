Thank you for giving this app a try!

This software uses AI (AskSage) to attempt to answer USSF and USAF questions utilizing AFIs, AFMANS, Local Supplements, and various DoD Authoritative Publications.

The app will not only answer DoD related questions, but also provide easily verifiable references for further inquiry and verification.


WARNING: The responses from this app should not be taken as authoritative truth. If in doubt, please continue to research the answer by other means.

This app is not affiliated with the Department of Defense or any of its branches.
